Allow admins to create nickname commands that apply dynamically to users or roles.

Commands

!setnickname [user] [nickname]

Instantly sets a user's nickname. Works with role hierarchy.

!resetnickname [user]

Resets a user's nickname to their default username.

!nicklog [user]

Shows nickname change history for a specific user.

!autorole [role]

Automatically assigns nicknames based on role.
